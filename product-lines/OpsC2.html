AOC BLOCK 20

BLOCK 20 is the future of the Air operation Center. OPSC2 is developing a suite of fully integrated applications that can sense and respond to conflict in any domain anytime, anywhere. Kessel Run has deployed several stand alone applications that are being used today in real-world Missions.

KRADOS

The Kessel Run All-Domain Operations Suite is the backbone of AOC Block 20. The applications within KRADOS are connected by common data streams and supported by a hybrid cloud infrastructure. This suite will enable Americaâ€™s warfighters to move faster and allow commanders to be more efficient with their manpower.


Slapshot

Slapshot builds the Master Air Attack Plan (MAAP). It supports the MAAP cell in the Combat Plans Division (CPD), and users in the Combat Operations Division (COD) around the world. The application is also used by joint coalition partners.


Jigsaw

Jigsaw provides an intuitive and reactive interface that enables tanker planners to save time, fuel and lives. The application has saved the DoD more than $500 Million in fuel costs, and was also provided to our partners at NATO.


Mai Tai

Mai Tai allows commanders to see what assets are available to them through accurate and secure communication. It standardizes data across Kessel Run applications to enable safer, continuous airpower through accurate and secure communication of asset data for mission planning and execution. It enables Kessel Run applications to plan in tandem and produce accurate Air Tasking Orders (ATOs).


Triton

Triton consumes information from the other applications to generate the Air Tasking Order (ATO). It provides accurate and timely Air Tasking Orders (ATOs) to empower Air Operation Center (AOC) warfighters with enhanced mission awareness through a flexible workflow.


Scout

Scout is a map tool, which displays geographical data. It accelerates Kessel Runâ€™s ability to deliver geospatial capabilities giving warfighters the right data and situational awareness to make decisions quickly and confidently.


KRID

KRID is the authentication tool used for accessing the KRADOS Suite of applications.


Spacer

Spacer is an air space management tool that coordinates and deconflicts air spaces within an area of responsibility. It allows Airspace Managers to increase operational effectiveness by providing safe, efficient, and flexible use of airspace.


Rebel Alliance

Rebel Alliance handles events within the KRADOS Suite, communicating data between the applications. It is the key to Kessel Run integrations, and supports all of our product teamsâ€™ efforts to share data. It pairs with teams to help improve their data models, define their aggregates, and integrate across Kessel Run.


Holonet

Holonet consumes live data from aircraft and makes it available to the KRADOS Suite of applications. It supports all Kessel Run applications integrated with Rebel Alliance or Rogue One. HoloNet will enable these applications to subscribe to the information sources they need so they can keep their users up to date with near real-time information.


Direct

Direct shows what the aircraft plan is, and keeps operators uptodate with any changes. It supports the Combat Operations Division, personnel conducting shift-change and other debriefs and any operator interested in mission situational awareness. Equips the warfighter with flexible mission monitoring and status tracking capability.

MODELING & SIMULATION

Modeling and Simulation supports combat readiness by providing the warfighter with a synthetic environment that is tailorable, scaleable, and accurate. This is pivotal for the modernization of system validation and operator training.
